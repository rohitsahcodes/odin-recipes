Baked French Fries Recipes

description:

Baked french fries are a healthier, lower-calorie alternative to deep-fried potatoes, fearuting a crispy exterior and tender interior.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large baking potato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon chilli powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients. Pre-heat the ovan to 450 degree F (230 degree C)
  2. Cut potato into thick wedges.
  3. Mix olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder together in a medium bowl. Add potato wedges and toss to coat evenly; arrange on a baking sheer.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven, turning once or twice, until golder brown and cripsy, about 45 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
