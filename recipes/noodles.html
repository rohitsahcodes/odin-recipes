Cabbage and Egg Noodles

***** 4.5(313)_______252 REVIEWS_______19 PHOTOS


This is a German recipe for fried cabbage and noodles that belonged to
my grandmother. It's cabbage that is lightly browned in butter and tossed
with egg noogles for a wonderful, hearty, and fast dish.

  • 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 medium head green cabbage, chopped
  • salt and pepper tp taste

Directions

Step 1

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add egg noodles and
cook until the pasta is tender yet firm to the bite, about 5 minutes; drain.


Step 2

Meanwhile, melt butter in a large skillet over low heat. Add cabbage and
season with salt and peper. Cover and cook until the cabbage begins to
brown, 5 to 7 minutes. Add cooked noodles:
cook and stir until the noodles begin to brown, about 5 minuttes.
